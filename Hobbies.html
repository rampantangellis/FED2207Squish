Biography

Roland Larsen was born in Marietta, GA. He grew up in a myriad of locations. One of which was Minnesota. He served in the United States Army for 8 years on active duty. He is married with 3 children and one on the way.

Table Top Games

At the age of 9, his brother came back down from Minnesota. It was at this time that he was introduced to the world of Magic the Gathering. So from the time he was 9 to now he plays this table top game even though it is virtually. This led to his interest in other table top games such as Dungeons and Dragons. He still plays these games simply because they require a thought process and an imagination.

Video Games
